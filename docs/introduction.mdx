## What is GAIA?

GAIA is an advanced personal AI assistant designed to enhance productivity and streamline daily tasks. With seamless integration capabilities and intelligent features, GAIA provides a comprehensive solution for managing your digital life.

**Website:** [heygaia.io](https://heygaia.io)

### Quick Start
The fastest way to experience GAIA is through our hosted service at [heygaia.io](https://heygaia.io). No installation required - just sign up and start using your AI assistant immediately.

### Self-Hosting
Perfect for users who want complete control over their data and deployment. Our Docker-based setup makes it easy to run GAIA on your own infrastructure with enterprise-grade security and customization options.

### Development
For developers who want to contribute to GAIA or customize it for their specific needs. Includes comprehensive development environment setup, architecture documentation, and contribution guidelines.
